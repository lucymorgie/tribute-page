Carlton House Vaccination Centre

A lot of vaccines given, by some very hard-working people

A timeline of events

  • 8th December 2020 - first Covid-19 vaccine given in the UK
  • 14th December 2020 - Carlton House Vaccination centre opens in the re-purposed waiting room of a GP surgery, one of the first to mobilise
  • 1st January 2021 - 500 doses given to the elderly and most vulnerable
  • February 2021 - operational capacity ramped up due to new recording systems
  • 30th March 2021 - 50,000 doses given
  • April - July 2021 - popups all over the borough in mosques, churches, community spaces, shopping centres and on custom buses
  • September 2021 - Autumn booster campaign offers additional doses
  • December 2021 - 200,000 doses given
